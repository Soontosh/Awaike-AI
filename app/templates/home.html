About AWAIKE

Driving, the most common form of transportation in the modern world, has become an integral part of our society in the past century. However, as a result, automobile-related accidents and injuries are one of the top leading causes of death, many of which have been caused by sleepy drivers. That's why our team has come together to combat driver drowsiness by detecting it before consequences can arise, therefore creating safer roads for everyone.
