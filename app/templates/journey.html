Our Journey


We came a long from not knowing anything about AI to training an object detection neural network. We've learnt how to use python, Roboflow and YOLOv5 to train a custom computer vision model. We also learnt about HTML, CSS, Bootstrap to create a frontend for the model(this website), and Flask to integrate the frontend with our backend.

Our model went through multiple versions to create the most accurate and robust model. In order to improve the efficiency of the model we had to continually adjust various factors pertaining to the data set.
